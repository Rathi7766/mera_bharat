Kerala

Known as "God's Own Country," Kerala is famous for its scenic backwaters, lush greenery, and rich cultural heritage. Explore tranquil beaches, beautiful hill stations, and vibrant festivals in this tropical paradise.

More About Kerala

Kerala offers attractions like the serene backwaters of Alleppey, the tea plantations of Munnar, and traditional Kathakali performances. It’s a perfect mix of natural beauty and rich traditions.
