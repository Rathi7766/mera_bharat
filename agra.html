Agra

Agra is home to the iconic Taj Mahal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World. Known for its rich history and architectural wonders, Agra is a must-visit destination.

More About Agra

Explore the Agra Fort, a majestic fort built by the Mughals, and the beautiful Mehtab Bagh with its views of the Taj Mahal. Agra offers a glimpse into India’s grand Mughal history.
